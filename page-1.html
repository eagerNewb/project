Meet an Ickle Pickle

Charlie was born at St. Georges Hospital in Tooting. He spent 3 months in intensive & specialist care from the incredible clinical and nursing teams in the Neonatal Unit.

He is just one of the babies that are treated every year in Neonatal Units around the UK. The Ickle Pickles helps by raising funds for the Intensive Care equipment that keeps theses babies ablive.Take two minutes to watch the video - you wont be disapointed.
